OUR PROGRAM
Our 9-month program is divided into 12 modules.
The last module is a 6-weeks intensive Final Project where students
work on a real project with a real partner.
Our training takes place at Epitech (Brussels) every Sunday. During the week,
students are supported online in their self-study,
homework and peer learning.

OUR IMPACT

We graduated over 100 people in Belgium and more than 300 globally. In Belgium,
over 70% found and retained a high-quality job as a developer.
